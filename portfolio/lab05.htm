Stanford6dof


Stanford6dof is a function that plots the stanford robot arm in its full 6 degrees of freedom.
It uses non-standard parameters. E.g. stanford6dof( pi/2, pi/2, pi/2, pi/2, pi/2, pi/2, 2, 5, 'coordframe', 1)
